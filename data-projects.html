Exploratory Data Analysis with Python

PROJECT 1: Do movies with higher budget yield higher revenues?

In this project, I explore whether movies with higher budgets perform better in terms of revenue and amount of votes.

After some cleaning on the dataset, I explore the correlations between the variables using tools as the correlation matrix. I then narrow the analysis to the variables in scope (budget, revenue and votes) using scatter plots using both Matplotlib and Seaborn.

Then, to visualize how the three variables play together, I use the infamous "Bubble Chart", from the one and only Gapminder.

At the end, just for the sake of exercise, I turn the descriptive variables into numeric variables to check for any relevant correlation. This last step may fuel future analysis.

PROJECT 2: How much time do I spend working out?

I like to be active. I would bet I spend at least 2.5 hours a day involved into some kind of physical exercise. In this project, I explore my Apple Watch data to finally quantify the time I spend working out.
